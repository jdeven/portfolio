About

I am a Digital Product Designer based in Toronto. I am fascinated with product acquisition and development, including aspects of branding, design, usability and function. My roots stem from television and film.

My Process

My design process begins with understanding the user. Research, surveys and interviews. This will enable the definition of personas. When MVP requirements are satisfied, high level designs follow, in the form of sketches, wireframing and mockups. Prototyping is the next step, alongside rigorous testing. When the product is shipped, analysis can begin again, with follow-up research and feedback from the user.
